# Business Metrics Framework

## Purpose

This document defines all proprietary business metrics used in the Mumbai Rent Intelligence Platform.

The objective is to convert raw rental data into business-friendly decision metrics.

---

## Metric 1

Mumbai Rental Value Index (MRVI)

Business Question

"Which locality offers the best rental value?"

Inputs

- Average Rent
- Average Rent per SqFt
- Average Carpet Area

Output

0–100 Score

Purpose

Help renters identify value-for-money localities.

---

## Metric 2

Market Premium Score (MPS)

Business Question

"How premium is this locality?"

Inputs

- Average Rent
- Average Rent per SqFt

Output

0–100 Score

Purpose

Identify luxury markets.

---

## Metric 3

Fair Rent Score (FRS)

Business Question

"Is this locality fairly priced?"

Inputs

- Rent
- Carpet Area
- Rent per SqFt

Output

Fair
Underpriced
Overpriced

Purpose

Identify pricing opportunities.
